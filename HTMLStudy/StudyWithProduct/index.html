Welcome to the Fruit Supermarket

Apple

Apple is one of the fruits, is a subfamily of the genus Rosaceae, and its tree is deciduous. The fruit of apples is rich in minerals and vitamins and is one of the fruits that people often eat.

Watermelon

Watermelon is the fruit of summer, the flesh is sweet, and it can cool down to the heat; the seed contains oil and can be used as a recreational food; the peel is medicinal, and it has the effects of clearing heat, diuresis and lowering blood pressure.

Pear

The fruit of pear is usually used for eating. It is not only delicious, but also sweet and sour. It is rich in nutrients and contains many kinds of vitamins and cellulose. Different kinds of pears have different taste and texture. Pears can be eaten raw or cooked. In terms of medical efficacy, pears can be used for constipation, digestive digestion, and cardiovascular benefits.

Grape

Grape is one of the oldest fruit tree species in the world. Grapes are famous fruits, raw food or raisins, and winemaking. After winemaking, the wine can be used to raise alcohol, and the roots and vines can stop vomiting and fetus.
